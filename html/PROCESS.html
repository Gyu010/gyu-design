GYU DESIGN PROCESS

01. Estimate Inquiry

  • Please complete the pre-consultation questionnaire with the required basic information.
  • After submission, we will contact you by phone within 2–3 business days, in order of receipt.
  • Quotations are provided through an in-office visit; phone consultations are very limited.
  • Following the initial office consultation, a preliminary estimate will be sent via email.
  • Preliminary estimates may vary depending on site conditions, selected finishes, level of detailing, and scope of work.
  • For on-site visits prior to contract signing, a nominal site visit fee applies and will be refunded upon confirmation.

02. Preliminary Contract

  • The preliminary contract corresponds to the design agreement stage.
  • GYU DESIGN’s average design period is approximately 4–5 weeks, and may be longer for larger projects.
  • The average construction period is typically 7–8 weeks, with extended timelines for large-scale projects.
  • We follow a structured construction sequence and do not pursue compressed schedules that compromise quality.
  • To achieve a high level of satisfaction, investing sufficient time is as important as the financial investment itself.

03. Design Meetings

  • Following on-site measurement, design meetings are held to define the detailed scope of work.
  • A meeting schedule is provided, including a structural meeting, color and concept meeting, finishes meeting, and final briefing with contract execution.
  • Design communication is conducted using SketchUp throughout the meeting process.
  • Gyu Design is not a firm dedicated solely to design services.
  • Upon signing the design agreement, the construction schedule is confirmed simultaneously.

04. Construction Preparation

  • All design plans are finalized two weeks prior to the start of construction.
  • Based on the completed design discussions, detailed drawings are prepared and the full construction schedule is confirmed.
  • Construction permits and resident consent procedures are handled through the building management office.

05. Construction Phase

  • Construction proceeds according to the final design confirmed during meetings.
  • Unavoidable on-site variables may result in partial adjustments through consultation with the client.
  • Three to four on-site meetings may be conducted throughout the construction period to ensure optimal results and client satisfaction.

06. Completion & After Service

  • The defect liability period is one year.
    Should any issues arise after completion, we are committed to providing prompt and reliable after-service support.
